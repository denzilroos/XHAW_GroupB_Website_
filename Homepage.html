Empowering the Nation

Found by Precious Radebe,Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg.
Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programming to empower themselves and can provide more marketable skills.
